### **Viki ‚Äî One-Time Activation Required**
The extension works on Viki with a simple first time setup:

1. Open any drama/video on Viki.  
2. **Pause** the video.  
3. Open your browser's **Extensions Page**.  
4. Click the **Reload** icon on this extension.

After this, Viki subtitles will always appear at the top automatically.
